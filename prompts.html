Gratitude Prompts

Some days gratitude comes easily, and other days it takes a little more thought. These prompts are here to help you slow down, reflect, and notice the beauty in your life, even in small and simple things.

Today's Gentle Reminders

Use these prompts whenever you need a little inspiration.

Prompt 01

What is one small moment from today that made you smile?

Prompt 02

Who is someone in your life you are thankful for, and why?

Prompt 03

What is something about your current season of life that you appreciate?

Prompt 04

What is one challenge that taught you something meaningful?

Prompt 05

What is something beautiful you saw recently?

Prompt 06

What is one thing your body allowed you to do today that you are grateful for?

Prompt 07

What is one memory you return to often because it brings you peace or joy?

Prompt 08

What is something in your everyday routine that quietly makes your life better?

Pause and Notice

Gratitude does not always have to be big. Sometimes it is just warm light, a kind text, a calm moment, a good laugh, or simply making it through the day.
